About Me

Hi there! I'm Rebecca, a nature and landscape photographer wth a passion for capturing the beauty of woodland areas and sweeping landscapes. My journey into photography began with simple hikes and a love for the outdoors, but it quickly evolved into a deeper appreciation for the natural world and a desire to share it's hidden wonders through my lens. I believe every photograph tells a story and I strive to bring viewers a sense of tranquility and connection to nature, inviting them to see the world as I do; full of quiet beauty and endless adventure. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new trails, searching for unique landscapes, or enjoying the serenity of a woodland walk. Whether you're here to explore my work, learn more about my journey, or even discuss a porential project, I'm glad you're here!
